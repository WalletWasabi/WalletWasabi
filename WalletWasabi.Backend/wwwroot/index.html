Reclaim your privacy now!

Wasabi is an open-source, non-custodial, privacy focused Bitcoin wallet for desktop. It implements trustless coin shuffling: Chaumian CoinJoin.

However, "anonymity loves company", the more participants there are, the better your privacy is, and the faster the CoinJoin rounds are. Whether you are looking for state of the art operational security or you are philosophically aligned with the principles of freedom and privacy, now it is YOUR time to contribute. Fire up your Wasabi and help us to bootstrap the system!
